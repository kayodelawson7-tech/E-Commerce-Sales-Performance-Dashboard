# E-Commerce-Sales-Performance-Dashboard
 ![Dashboard](E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard1)
 
The E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI to analyze the sales performance of an online retail business. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of key business metrics such as revenue, customer count, orders, product performance, sales trends, payment methods, referral sources, and coupon usage.
The purpose of this dashboard is to help business managers make informed decisions by identifying high-performing products, 
understanding customer purchasing behavior, and evaluating different marketing and payment channels.

## Project Objectives
The main objectives of this project are:
•	To monitor overall sales performance. 
•	To analyze revenue generated by different products. 
•	To identify the best-selling products. 
•	To study monthly sales trends. 
•	To compare payment methods used by customers. 
•	To evaluate referral sources that generate sales. 
•	To analyze coupon code usage among customers. 
•	To support business decision-making using data visualization

## Dashboard Visualizations Analysis 
### Total Revenue by Product
This horizontal bar chart compares the revenue generated by each product.

## Observations
•	Chairs generated the highest revenue. 
•	Printers and laptops also contributed significantly. 
•	Phones generated comparatively lower revenue.

### Business Insight
The company should continue investing in products that generate the highest revenue while developing strategies to increase sales for lower-performing products.

### Quantity Sold by Product
This column chart displays the quantity sold for each product category.

## Observations
•	Chairs recorded the highest number of units sold. 
•	Printers and laptops followed closely. 
•	Phones had the lowest quantity sold. 
Business Insight
Products with high sales volume indicate strong customer demand and should receive priority in inventory management.
Revenue by Payment Method and Product
This stacked percentage column chart shows how different products contribute to revenue across payment methods.
Payment Methods Included
•	Online Payment 
•	Cash 
•	Credit Card 
•	Debit Card 
•	Gift Card 

## Observations
•	Revenue distribution is relatively balanced across all payment methods. 
•	Online and card payments contribute a significant share of sales. 
•	Customers utilize multiple payment options rather than relying on a single method.

## Business Insight
Providing diverse payment methods improves customer convenience and may increase sales.

## Monthly Revenue Trend
The line chart illustrates monthly revenue over time.

## Observations
•	Revenue fluctuates throughout the reporting period. 
•	Several months recorded noticeable revenue peaks. 
•	Temporary declines are followed by periods of recovery. 

## Business Insight
Sales are seasonal in nature. Management can investigate promotional campaigns or external factors responsible for revenue spikes and apply similar strategies during slower months.

## Revenue by Referral Source

Referral Sources
•	Instagram 
•	Email

## Observations
Revenue contributions are relatively balanced among all referral sources.

## Customer Distribution by Coupon Code

Coupon Codes
•	FREESHIP 
•	No Coupon 
•	WINTER15 
•	SAVE10 

## Observations
•	Coupon usage is fairly evenly distributed. 
•	FREESHIP appears to be the most popular promotion. 
•	Many customers still purchase without applying any coupon. 
Business Insight
Free shipping promotions are highly attractive and can be used strategically to increase customer purchases.

 ## Overall Business Insights
Based on the dashboard analysis:
•	The business generated $1.26 million in total revenue. 
•	Around 1,189 customers placed approximately 1,200 orders. 
•	Nearly 4,000 products were sold. 
•	Chairs are both the highest revenue-generating and best-selling product. 
•	Phones contribute the least to overall sales performance. 
•	Multiple payment methods are effectively utilized by customers. 
•	Revenue experiences periodic fluctuations, indicating seasonal purchasing behavior. 
•	Marketing channels contribute relatively evenly to revenue generation. 
•	Coupon campaigns, especially FREESHIP, encourage customer purchases. 

## Recommendations
Based on the findings, the following recommendations are suggested:
1.	Increase inventory for high-demand products such as Chairs and Printers. 
2.	Develop promotional campaigns to improve sales of Phones. 
3.	Expand digital marketing efforts on the best-performing referral channels. 
4.	Continue offering multiple payment options to enhance customer satisfaction. 
5.	Use seasonal promotions during periods of lower sales. 
6.	Introduce targeted coupon campaigns to increase customer retention and repeat purchases. 
7.	Monitor monthly revenue trends regularly to identify emerging sales opportunities.

## Conclusion
The E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard provides a clear and interactive view of the organization's sales performance. It consolidates key metrics into a single dashboard, enabling managers to monitor revenue, customer activity, product performance, payment preferences, marketing effectiveness, and promotional campaigns.
The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by highlighting sales trends, identifying top-performing products, and revealing opportunities for business growth. By leveraging Power BI's interactive features and visual analytics, this solution helps stakeholders quickly understand business performance and implement strategies to improve revenue, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.
